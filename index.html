Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how RobustProg ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile applications available on Google Play Store.

Last updated: June 8, 2025

1 Information We Collect

What We DON'T Collect

We do NOT directly collect, store, or process any personal information, user data, or device information through our applications.

Third-Party Services

Our apps use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process certain information, including:

  • Advertising ID (AAID) for Android devices
  • Device information for ad personalization
  • Usage data for ad optimization

2 How Information is Used

By Our Apps

We do not use any collected information as we do not collect user data directly through our applications.

By AdMob

Google AdMob uses collected information to provide personalized advertisements and improve ad performance.

3 Information Sharing

Since we do not collect personal information directly, we do not share, sell, or rent personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

Information collected by Google AdMob is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. For detailed information about how AdMob handles data, please visit:

Google Privacy Policy

4 Your Rights and Choices

Advertising Preferences

You can opt-out of personalized advertising through your device settings or Google Ad Settings.

Reset Advertising ID

You can reset your advertising ID at any time through your Android device settings.

App Uninstallation

You can uninstall our apps at any time, which will stop any data collection by AdMob for those specific apps.

5 Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal information directly, there is minimal risk to your data through our applications. However, we are committed to ensuring that any third-party services we use (such as AdMob) maintain appropriate security measures.

Third-Party Security

Google AdMob implements industry-standard security measures to protect the information they collect. For more details, please refer to Google's Security and Privacy documentation.

6 Children's Privacy

Our applications do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. Since we do not collect personal information directly, our apps are generally safe for children to use.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided personal information through third-party services in our apps, please contact us immediately.

7 Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
